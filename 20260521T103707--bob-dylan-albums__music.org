#+title:      Bob Dylan Albums
#+date:       [2026-05-21 Do 10:37]
#+filetags:   :music:
#+identifier: 20260521T103707

* The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan (1963): 
His definitive folk statement. This album established him as the voice of a generation with anthems like "Blowin' in the Wind".

* Highway 61 Revisited (1965): 
The ultimate electric album. It revolutionized rock and features "Like a Rolling Stone," frequently cited as one of the greatest songs ever recorded.

* Bringing It All Back Home (1965): 
The pivotal transition record where Dylan went electric, featuring folk-rock staples like "Subterranean Homesick Blues".

* Blonde on Blonde (1966): 
A sprawling, double-album masterpiece that perfectly blends surrealist lyrics with a high-energy rock and blues sound.

* Blood on the Tracks (1975): 
A deeply personal, acoustic-driven masterpiece born from his marital separation. It stands as his most emotionally resonant and widely praised record.

* Time Out of Mind (1997): 
A haunting, Grammy-winning late-career triumph that defined his modern era
